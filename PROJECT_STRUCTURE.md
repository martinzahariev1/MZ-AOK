# Project Structure

The repository is organized as an Evidence Management System. Main folders and important subfolders follow the multilingual naming format:

`EN name - BG name - DE name`

## Main Folders

- `00_Case Dashboard - Табло на случая - Fallübersicht`: indexes, CSV trackers, and working dashboards.
- `01_Timeline - Хронология - Zeitlinie`: chronological event records and timeline notes.
- `02_Evidence - Доказателства - Beweismittel`: source evidence files and evidence summaries.
- `03_Documents - Документи - Dokumente`: formal documents organized by source or category.
- `04_Entities - Лица и фирми - Personen und Firmen`: people, companies, institutions, and relationships.
- `05_Finance - Финанси - Finanzen`: payments, deductions, claims, invoices, and financial analysis.
- `06_Legal - Правни въпроси - Recht`: legal arguments, filings, laws, deadlines, and correspondence.
- `07_Analysis - Анализ - Analyse`: findings, comparisons, contradictions, and investigative notes.
- `08_Reports - Доклади - Berichte`: prepared reports, summaries, exports, and case packets.
- `09_Automation - Автоматизация - Automatisierung`: scripts, templates, prompts, and automation outputs.
- `10_Archive - Архив - Archiv`: inactive, superseded, or preserved historical material.
