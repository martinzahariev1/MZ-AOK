# 02_Evidence - Доказателства - Beweismittel

## What Belongs Here

Primary evidence files, screenshots, scans, source exports, photos, recordings, and evidence summaries.

## Naming Rules

Preserve originals and label copies or annotations clearly:

`YYYY-MM-DD_Source_Type_Short-description_Language_Status.ext`

## Examples

- `2026-06-24_Bank_Statement_Transaction-list_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-06-24_WhatsApp_Screenshot_Payment-request_BG_copy.png`
