# Tax Office - Данъчна служба - Finanzamt

## What Belongs Here

Tax office letters, assessments, filings, tax numbers, payment notices, and Finanzamt correspondence.

## Naming Rules

Include date, office, document type, tax period or subject, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-04-30_Finanzamt_Assessment_Income-tax-2025_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-05-15_Finanzamt_Letter_Tax-number_DE_original.pdf`
