# Invoices - Фактури - Rechnungen

## What Belongs Here

Issued invoices, received invoices, invoice corrections, credit notes, and invoice summaries.

## Naming Rules

Include date, issuer, invoice type, invoice number or subject, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-03-31_Partner-Company_Invoice_INV-2026-031_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-04-15_Hermes_Credit-note_Adjustment_DE_copy.pdf`
