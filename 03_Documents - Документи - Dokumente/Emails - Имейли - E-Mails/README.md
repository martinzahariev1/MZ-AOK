# Emails - Имейли - E-Mails

## What Belongs Here

Email files, PDF exports of emails, attachments, email threads, and email summaries.

## Naming Rules

Use sent date, sender, email type, subject summary, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-06-24_AOK_Email_Insurance-status_DE_original.eml`
- `2026-06-25_Accountant_Email_Payroll-question_DE_original.pdf`
