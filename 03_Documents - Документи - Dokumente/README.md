# 03_Documents - Документи - Dokumente

## What Belongs Here

Formal documents grouped by source, institution, business category, or communication channel.

## Naming Rules

Place documents in the most specific subfolder and use:

`YYYY-MM-DD_Source_Type_Short-description_Language_Status.ext`

## Examples

- `2026-06-20_AOK_Letter_Insurance-demand_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-06-21_Partner-Company_Invoice_Service-fee_DE_original.pdf`
