# Bank - Банка - Bank

## What Belongs Here

Bank statements, transaction exports, payment confirmations, account letters, and bank correspondence.

## Naming Rules

Use statement period or transaction date first.

## Examples

- `2026-05-31_Bank_Statement_May-2026_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-06-05_Bank_Payment-confirmation_AOK-transfer_DE_original.pdf`
