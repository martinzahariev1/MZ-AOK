# Health Insurance - Здравни каси - Krankenkassen

## What Belongs Here

Health insurance letters, contribution notices, coverage documents, payment demands, and correspondence with Krankenkassen.

## Naming Rules

Include date, health insurer, document type, short subject, language, and status.
