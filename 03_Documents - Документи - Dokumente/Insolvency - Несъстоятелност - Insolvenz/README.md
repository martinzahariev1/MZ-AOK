# Insolvency - Несъстоятелност - Insolvenz

## What Belongs Here

Insolvency filings, administrator letters, creditor notices, claim registrations, and deadline notes.

## Naming Rules

Include date, insolvency office or administrator, document type, subject, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-05-20_Insolvency-Admin_Letter_Claim-registration_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-05-25_Creditor-Portal_Form_Claim-submission_DE_final.pdf`
