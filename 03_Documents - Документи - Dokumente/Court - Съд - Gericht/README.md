# Court - Съд - Gericht

## What Belongs Here

Court filings, orders, decisions, notices, hearing records, and court correspondence.

## Naming Rules

Include date, court, filing type, short subject, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-06-10_Court_Filing_Objection_DE_final.pdf`
- `2026-06-18_Court_Order_Hearing-date_DE_original.pdf`
