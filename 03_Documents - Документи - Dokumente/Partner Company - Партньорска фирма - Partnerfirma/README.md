# Partner Company - Партньорска фирма - Partnerfirma

## What Belongs Here

Documents from or about a partner company, including agreements, invoices, correspondence, and disputes.

## Naming Rules

Include date, company name, document type, subject, language, and status.

## Examples

- `2026-03-01_Partner-Company_Agreement_Service-terms_DE_original.pdf`
- `2026-04-12_Partner-Company_Email_Invoice-dispute_DE_original.eml`
